MR/MS INVESTIGATOR

Get ready to dive into a thrilling adventure where you become the detective! In this exciting event, teams will tackle a perplexing mystery filled with scenarios, Crimes, quirky clues, and eyewitness reports. You'll need sharp thinking, creativity, and a knack for solving riddles to crack the evidence in the case. But the fun doesn’t stop there – in the ACCESS DENIED ! round, the challenge shifts to a digital mystery! Can you uncover hidden passwords and unlock secret accounts using just a few clever tricks? It’s a race against time as you move from investigating the real world to navigating the digital maze. The team that connects all the dots, both in the physical clues and the online puzzles, will claim the prestigious title of MR. or MS. Investigator, along with bragging rights as the smartest detectives around!
